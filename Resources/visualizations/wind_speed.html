Wind Speed

Majority of the cities measured has wind speeds less than 15 mph at the time of data retrieval. The regression analysis on both the hemispheres leads to better understanding!

  • Windspeed doesn't correlate with the distance from the equator.
    • Please observe that p-value of the linear regression estimator > significance level (typically 0.05). This means that WE CANNOT say that slope is NOT zero.
    • In both hemispheres, a weak correlation between latitude and Windspeed.
  • At the time of data is taken, wind speed is centred around almost the same value in both hemispheres.
    • Northern hemisphere has average windspeed around 6.9 mph.
    • Southern hemisphere has average windspeed around 7.1 mph.
